# Demo source video credits

The defense demo stores source clips locally so it works without a network connection.

## Chinese license plate footage

- File: `assets/license_plate_source.webm`
- Wikimedia Commons title: `The car ahead turned around and drove in the opposite direction at the Shanghai elevated exit.webm`
- Scene: dashcam footage from a Shanghai elevated-road exit; the target vehicle uses a Chinese mainland blue license plate.
- License status on Wikimedia Commons: Public domain; attribution not required.
- Source: `https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:The_car_ahead_turned_around_and_drove_in_the_opposite_direction_at_the_Shanghai_elevated_exit.webm`
- Modifications: trimmed, resized, letterboxed, and annotated by the existing HyperLPR cascade plus a blue-plate color filter.
- HyperLPR cascade: copied from the existing MiniMover plate module; Apache License 2.0 text is stored as `assets/HYPERLPR_LICENSE.txt`.
